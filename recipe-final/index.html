Borscht

Yield 12 servings
Time 2.5 hrs
  • Beef or Pork, 2 pounds
  • 1 14.5 oz can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 young beets, with tops
  • 3 carrots, thinly sliced or grated
  • 2 stalks celery, thinly sliced or grated
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1/4 head of green cabbage, shredded
  • 4 cups water, boiling
  • Salt (or bullion), to taste
  • Paprika, to taste
  1. Dredge meat in seasoned flour and brown in stock pot
  2. Add tomatoes (with juice) and water
  3. Bring to a boil, then cover and simmer
  4. Separate beets from tops, peel beets, slice and throw them in the pot
  5. Saut√© the onion, clerey and carrots; throw them in the pot
  6. Shred the cabbage; throw it in the pot
  7. Chop up the beet greens, then add them to the pot
  8. Simmer a total of 2 hours from when the meat first started to simmer (or until the meat is done)
  9. Add salt and paprika, to taste
  10. Serve with dill and sour cream
Enjoy!
